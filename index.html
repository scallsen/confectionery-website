Our Promise

Cho’s has been providing top quality chocolates to the Ottawa region for over 150 years. Our ChocoMasters have years in experience working at our other locations worldwide: from France to Belgium, and it shows! Every individual cube of chocolate has undergone a rigorous testing process to ensure you get the absolute best top quality cocoa based treat, every time.

The Process

We only use the finest ingredients on traditional Japanese Oak wood tables from a hidden grove in Kyoto, Japan. We bake our chocolate over lava powered furnaces gathered from the depths of the forgotten mines of Mt. Fuji. Our cocoa is gathered from a long forgotten tribe in the Amazonian Jungle with a long-forgotten recipe. Come taste the difference our chocolate makes.
